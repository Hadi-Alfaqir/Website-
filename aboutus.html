About Us

The focus of Eventus was initially to develop marketing products for companies that are in the SME phase (small to medium enterprises). By the year 2015, Eventus then rebranded itself; this eventually resulted in a turnover that was even higher than before which was already substantial. Despite initial losses in year one, Eventus managed to reform and is on a high growth trajectory with profits constantly rising. For the sustainable growth, the focus of Eventus is to innovate on a regular basis by using the strategy of product differentiation. Based on the strategy, Eventus is constantly growing and increasing in its competitiveness while also being sustainable since it is constantly evolving and innovating.
